About us

Galeria Pieczywa - Staropolski Smak Chleba is a bakery founded in 2012, with a deep-rooted commitment to delivering the authentic taste of traditional Polish bread. With a strong emphasis on quality, this bakery takes pride in sourcing the finest ingredients and employing time-honored baking techniques to craft bread that is truly exceptional in taste and texture.



We are currently located in 2 locations across Warsaw, Poland, but we want to expand the company further. Our latest venture is an online platform, Galeria Pieczywa - Staropolski Smak Chleba, where customers can conveniently order our high-quality bread products from the comfort of their homes and have them delivered right to their doors. Feel free to explore the website to learn more about us.
